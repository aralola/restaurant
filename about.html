Aralola's Food Arcade

OUR STORY

Aralola's Food Arcade, a full service-catering outfit, is specialized in the exquisite art of unveiling African cuisines in its finest forms and tastes.

Aralola's Food Arcade is suitably designed to suit your exquisite and luxury functions. It offers an exclusive private dining room that seats 20 persons. Its main restaurant seats 170 persons in multi-level sections. There is also a sit-out for alfresco dining on the premises.

We have 2 locations: one in Yaba,Lagos State and the other in Ijebu Ode,Ogun State.
